Scoring Methodology

FCTO Signal scores deals across 8 weighted dimensions to determine whether a Field CTO should be involved.

DimensionWeightWhat it measures
Executive Access25%Stakeholder seniority, CTO/CIO involvement, decision-maker count
Technical Complexity20%Platform complexity, integration depth, headless architecture, channel scope
Stakeholder Skepticism15%Org receptivity to vendor input
Migration Risk15%Platform risk × urgency × geographic scope
Deal Scale10%GMV tier and deal stage maturity
Competitive Pressure8%Competitive intensity and specific competitor present
Digital Maturity4%Nature of customer objections raised
Industry Specificity3%Vertical-specific complexity

Priority Tiers

  • Monitor (0–30): Standard sales motion, no FCTO needed now
  • Consider (31–55): Targeted FCTO touchpoint may accelerate deal
  • Engage (56–75): FCTO actively participates in key conversations
  • Prioritize (76–100): FCTO-led deal, high-leverage opportunity

"Don't know yet" handling

Selecting "Don't know yet" scores conservatively high, reflecting likely FCTO need rather than assuming low complexity.
